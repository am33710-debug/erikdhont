Erik Dhont is a Landscape Architect based in Brussels, Geneva and Milan. Since the beginning of his practice in 1989 he has worked on a variety of projects.

His portfolio includes many projects across the Europe and the USA, such as gardens, farmyards, urban developments, etc.

Erik Dhont sees garden design as a form of art as they are places to enjoy and appreciate life.
